Decades of Sumptuous Tradition

At Swagath, we take immense pride in our profound heritage steeped in culinary tradition and the art of delivering delicious flavours. For years, we've been the torchbearers of an exquisite culinary journey that showcases the rich and diverse tapestry of Indian and Continental cuisine. Our story is one of passion, authenticity, and an unwavering commitment to offering our guests a dining experience that transcends time.

1967-1973

The Humble Beginnings in Mumbai

Jayaram Banan's culinary journey started in 1967 when he arrived in Mumbai, a bustling city known for its diverse food culture. At 13, he left his home in Uddapi, Karnataka, and embarked on a daring adventure with limited resources. Despite his young age, he found employment as a hotel worker, taking on menial tasks such as washing and cleaning. Over the next few years, he persevered and worked his way up, eventually becoming a manager. This period marked his initial encounter with the hospitality industry and laid the foundation for his future success.

About Us

1980s

Relocation to Delhi and Entrepreneurial Ventures

In the late 1970s, Jayaram Banan relocated to Delhi, where he faced the fierce competition of the culinary world. Undeterred, he secured a tender for the canteen of Central Electronics, demonstrating his entrepreneurial acumen. Saving diligently, he opened his small South Indian restaurant in Delhi, marking the beginning of his independent culinary venture.
